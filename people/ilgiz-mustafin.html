Ilgiz Mustafin

Ilgiz Mustafin is a PhD Student at the Chair of Software Engineering, Constructor Institute of Technology.

He is focused on software correctness, with a strong commitment to applying the latest tools in industry.

Research interests of Ilgiz center on finding practical solutions which are applicable in the daily work of software engineers.

His latest work is connected with AutoProof, a static program verifier for Eiffel which uses Boogie and Z3, and Bugfix, a multi-lingual automatic program repair effort.

Ilgiz also maintains Reif, the research version of EiffelStudio.
